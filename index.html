About Ian

Ian came here to eat hot wings and develop your web — and he's all out of hot wings. Ian comes to web development from a background in K-12 education, which honed his skills in boiling down difficult concepts and communicating them. When he became a father in 2022, he decided he needed to pursue a career from home. Now he's tackling divs and scripts instead of lesson plans and gradebooks, and is ready to work on all your web development needs.
